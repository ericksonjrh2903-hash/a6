Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

At Timeless Bag Closet, accessible from timelessbagcloset.com, protecting visitor privacy is a fundamental pillar of our operations. This Privacy Policy details the types of data collected and recorded by our platform and how we utilize it.

For any inquiries regarding this policy or data protection, please contact our team at privacy@timelessbagcloset.com.

1. Information Categories Collected

We process personal and non-personal data for legitimate operational needs:

  • Personal Data: Collected when you fill out contact forms, subscribe to our leather registry, or request bespoke handbag monograms. This includes your name, email address, delivery location, and contact phone number.
  • Usage & Device Analytics: Collected automatically through web hosting logs. This includes your IP address, browser type, device specs, referring pages, and timestamp metrics.
  • Cookie Data: Sourced via cookies to remember user preferences, maintain session state, and deliver optimized layout performance.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Timeless Bag Closet processes collected data to:

  • Provide, run, and optimize our digital handbag showroom and acquisition services.
  • Analyze user interaction to expand our leather care guides and handbag catalogs.
  • Communicate limited edition handbag releases, newsletters, and leather spa dates.
  • Serve relevant, non-intrusive ads through platforms like Google Ads.
  • Detect, prevent, and address system vulnerabilities, unauthorized access, or fraudulent transactions.

3. Log File Analysis

Timeless Bag Closet follows industry standards by maintaining log files. These files document visitor traffic when accessing the site. This is a default practice of hosting servers and forms part of hosting analytics. Information includes IP addresses, browser client, ISP details, date stamps, and referring/exit coordinates. This data is fully aggregated and is not linked to any personally identifiable information, serving only to monitor demographic layout and web performance.

4. Third-Party Advertising & Tracking Policies

We work with trusted marketing partners, including Google Ads, who serve cookies and web beacons on our site. These third-party tracking services collect aggregated data to analyze marketing performance and serve personalized advertising based on your previous browsing history.

5. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you possess specific data protection rights, including: the right to access stored data, the right to edit inaccuracies, the right to request deletion ("right to be forgotten"), the right to object to data processing, and the right to port your data. To exercise these rights, please email us directly.
